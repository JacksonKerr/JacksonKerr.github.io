Evan Monsma and this site - Written 2025-01-23

In the last year or so I have made an effort to change the type of content I have been consuming. switch to content that was slower and focused on the real world. This was imspored by being suggested one of Evan Monsma's videos I Dropped Out and Moved Into a 100 Year Old Cellar.

The video and audio quality are not great but the vibe is relaxing and he tells a story.

I think his videos do the best job of expressing the vibe I want to capture in this website.

I also want to try and present some of my hobbies here in a low-effort and caual way. In the past I have held high standards for what constitutes as being "worth sharing" and this has lead to not releasing anything I make. Perfectionism is the enemy of just getting something out there. Evan uses the end-tag "Made for the sake of making".
